About

My philosophy

Great design comes from wanting to create value and make a positive difference
— even in situations where it isn't obvious — in someone's life.
Whether it's a user I'm designing for or a teammate I'm working with, I'm determined to truly understand perspectives and needs that are different from my own.

Being involved in the entire design process, from strategy to implementation
is something I am looking for in my career.


Currently

My current role as a Product designer at Bower, involves owning and optimizing the Bower app with the aim of increasing the activation rate, enhancing user satisfaction, and driving positive behavioral changes towards recycling.

Before joining Bower, I gained valuable experience as a Product Design at KPMG, where I worked on designing high-impact customer experience strategies to drive results.


For fun

I'm based in Stockholm but originally hail from Florence, Italy. I love anything related to travel and food. Travel opens up my mind and spirit to new cultures, people, and experiences. I like to express my creativity by cooking in my spare time.
